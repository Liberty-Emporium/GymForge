Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a credit card to start the free trial?

No. Your 14-day free trial starts the moment you complete setup. No payment information required until you choose to subscribe.

How long does setup take?

Most gyms are fully live in under 10 minutes. The setup wizard walks you through gym details, locations, membership plans, and your owner account — step by step.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time from your Owner dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is yours. We provide a full export before account deletion. Data is retained for 30 days after cancellation in case you change your mind.

Is GymForge suitable for multi-location gyms?

Yes. The Enterprise plan supports unlimited locations under one account, with location-specific scheduling, staff, and reporting.
